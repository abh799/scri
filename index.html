About SCRI

SCRI

Amidst new waves of development, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi is aiming new heights with its vision, “To be a leader in science and technology education, knowledge creation and innovation, in an India marching towards a just, inclusive and sustainable society’’. IIT Mandi is continuously nurturing new avenues for promotion of research and innovation. It has given a launching pad to Society for Collaborative Research and Innovation (SCRI) which aims at providing a collaborative platform for extensive, effective and sustainable research and innovations. SCRI is a platform to provide good opportunities for students of any field or Discipline and it gives exposures to Interdisciplinary Research areas.

Photography and Movie Club in collaboration with SCRI presents Technical Talk Show- a series where the experiences and overall opinions of IIT Mandi faculty and student achievers will be captured. We have released our first episode which is Interaction with Dr. Ramesh Orunganti where he shares his views on current education systems, his experiences abroad and his visions for the future.We will slowly release episodes with all faculty and student achievers.

Science Day

The day was celebrated with “Excite Us with Science” competition where students of IIT Mandi presented us with exciting scientific research conducted at IIT Mandi in a 3 minutes time . Dr Timothy A. Gonsalves was scrutinised the performance of all the participants the chief guest. He inspired the audience with the glorified message on how science has been able to alter our lives for a better future. Dr. Viswanathan, Dr. Bindu and Dr. Pooja were the judges. Special thanks to faculty members & respected judges for taking out time from their busy schedule.
